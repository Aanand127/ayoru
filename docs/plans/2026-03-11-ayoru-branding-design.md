# Ayoru Branding Mock

**Date:** 2026-03-11

## Core Positioning

**Name:** Ayoru

**Primary Line:** A quieter way to watch anime.

**One-line Description:** Ayoru is a cleaner, safer way to watch anime with less friction.

## Brand Intent

Ayoru should feel calm, trustworthy, and deliberate.

It is not a loud anime brand, not a hacker toy, and not a flashy piracy identity. The project promise is that it removes friction and reduces the sketchiness users normally accept when trying to watch anime for free.

The brand should communicate:

- quieter
- cleaner
- safer
- more intentional
- more polished

The brand should avoid communicating:

- chaos
- neon overload
- edginess
- “pirate culture”
- terminal elitism

## Visual Direction

### Overall Mood

Dark premium, restraint, and confidence.

Think:

- soft media surfaces
- cool-neutral depth
- quiet navigation
- cinematic calm with subtle atmosphere
- Apple TV-like polish adapted for anime

Not:

- cyberpunk saturation
- anime expo poster energy
- retro hacker green-on-black
- heavy blue branding blocks
- mascot-led branding

### Color Palette

**Base**

- Graphite: `#16181C`
- Soft Charcoal: `#1D2127`
- Smoke: `#262C34`

**Text**

- Warm Silver: `#E7E8EA`
- Muted Silver: `#B8BEC7`
- Quiet Gray: `#8B93A1`

**Atmosphere**

- Steel Blue: `#5E748F`
- Mist Blue: `#8EA3BA`

**Usage**

- Surfaces should read neutral first, color second.
- Blue should feel atmospheric rather than like a dominant brand accent.
- Most of the system should rely on spacing, hierarchy, and depth rather than overt color.
- Borders should be low-contrast and slightly luminous.
- “Glass” should be subtle and lifted, not everywhere.

## Typography Direction

### Brand/Typography Personality

Use typography that feels elegant and composed rather than futuristic or playful.

Desired characteristics:

- narrow or slightly condensed headline feel
- strong readability
- soft sophistication
- no exaggerated techno shapes

### Suggested Type Pairing Direction

**Display / wordmark direction**

- something with cinematic tension and clean curves
- examples of the feel to explore: `Cormorant Garamond`, `Canela`, `PP Editorial New`, `IvyPresto`

**UI / body direction**

- restrained sans-serif
- examples of the feel to explore: `Manrope`, `Söhne`, `Neue Montreal`, `Geist`

This is a direction, not a locked implementation requirement.

## Logo Direction

### Recommended Approach

Wordmark-first.

The name should do most of the work.

### Logo Rules

- no mascot
- no anime face or silhouette
- no katana/shuriken/moon cliché pile-up
- no hard cyberpunk chrome effects

### Optional Symbol

If a symbol exists, keep it minimal:

- a horizon arc
- a moonline
- a quiet motion stroke
- a subtle “A” construction with night-sky geometry

## TUI Styling Mock

The TUI should feel understated, not busy.

### Direction

- soft borders
- quiet status copy
- cool-neutral highlights
- strong spacing
- implied depth rather than literal glass
- no rainbow accents
- no terminal gimmicks

### Header mock

```text
AYORU
A quieter way to watch anime.
```

### Status tone examples

- `Ready`
- `Searching`
- `Episodes loaded`
- `Playback failed`
- `Back to results`

Avoid jokey or overly clever terminal copy.

### Translation Rule

Because the TUI cannot do blur or real material:

- use softer contrast instead of loud accent color
- imply glass through depth and separation, not frosted effects
- let typography and spacing carry the premium feeling
- keep the interface denser and calmer than a typical terminal app

## macOS App Direction

The upcoming native app should extend the same identity rather than reinvent it.

### App icon direction

- dark neutral base
- subtle horizon or media-panel geometry
- one cool atmospheric accent only
- readable at small sizes

### Window styling direction

- dark soft surfaces
- strong typography hierarchy
- minimal chrome
- translucent layers where they improve depth
- one cool focus accent only

## Voice Guide

### Sound Like

- calm
- precise
- low-noise
- trustworthy

### Do Not Sound Like

- smug
- edgy
- chaotic
- memey
- self-consciously underground

### Good copy examples

- `A quieter way to watch anime.`
- `Cleaner playback with less friction.`
- `Search, choose, watch.`
- `Built to feel calmer than the usual alternatives.`

### Bad copy examples

- `The ultimate anime piracy weapon`
- `Watch anything anywhere with zero rules`
- `Terminal anime for power users only`
- `Destroy streaming friction`

## Recommendation

Lock the public-facing identity to:

- **Name:** Ayoru
- **Tagline:** A quieter way to watch anime.
- **Description:** A cleaner, safer way to watch anime with less friction.
- **Brand direction:** dark premium quiet trust

## Approval Questions

When reviewing this mock, the main decisions are:

1. Does `quiet trust` feel like the right identity?
2. Does the tagline stay strong enough for README, website, and app use?
3. Does the visual direction feel premium without becoming generic?
